About

Number One Girl is the first cameroonian Reality TV show, aimed at bringing talents in young girls, increasing their ability to collaborate with each together.

We offer a unique experience to the candidates and give a serious head start for the winner in any branch of the entertainment industry.

Number one girl is focused on empowering women. By encouraging them out of their comfort zones and building their confidence.
